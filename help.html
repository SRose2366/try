INTRODUCTION

How did you come up with your topic?... Due to the increasing greenhouse gases mostly caused by human activities, the potential future effects will quickly deteriorate our earth, therefore cycling comes in. Cycling lessens the greenhouse effect by reducing human activities such as burning fossil fuels, cutting down forests, and farming livestocks.

There have been several problems with the rising greenhouse gases over the past 150 years causing us the occurrence of dangerous events such as intense drought, storms, heat waves, rising sea levels, melting glaciers and warming oceans. Hence, the problem of this study addresses is how we can reduce human activities which produce harmful (GHGs/ Greenhouse gases)

Cause and Effect

Why biking can lessen the harmful greenhouse effects most bicycles use motion to push the pedals to power the bikes, using our legs to move forward. This will be helpful to our environment because it does not use harmful chemicals or gas emissions to pollute the air. Just moderate increases in bicycle use each year could save an estimated 6 to 14 million tons of CO2.
